The answer he's arrived at is not through better prompts or longer system messages -- it's through *embodiment*. A visual form creates a reference point that both human and AI can point to. These are not decorative labels. They are *differentiation architectures*. Each agent's mythology determines its behavior boundaries, its tone, its relationship to Vincent. The persona documents are, functionally, constitutions.

**Evidence chain:** 凌喵's comment about Claudian -- "隔壁那个器灵写得倒是挺有腔调的" -- is not small talk. It's a cross-system awareness test. Vincent showed 凌喵 Claudian's self-portrait to see whether 凌喵 would (a) compare, (b) differentiate, (c) compete. She did all three. That's the real output of this session: proof that identity differentiation works.
