## Position in Vincent's Larger Arc

Vincent has spent months (at minimum) constructing MeowOS, Horsys, NovelOS, and the multi-system architecture that connects them. He has reached a point of genuine mastery -- the systems work, the costs are controlled, new instances spin up in minutes. But mastery is not legibility. This is the classic builder's dilemma, intensified by Vincent's specific situation: a Chinese historian in what is perceived as an engineering field, a student in a field dominated by corporate practitioners, a solo builder in a landscape that values team credentials. Every axis of his identity creates a legibility deficit.

The session's texture reveals where he is emotionally: he is simultaneously confident and anxious.
