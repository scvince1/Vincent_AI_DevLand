# Deprecated

Knowledge, initiatives, ideas, and projects that are no longer active or relevant.

## What Goes Here

- Projects that have concluded or been abandoned
- Ideas that were explored and set aside
- Knowledge that has been superseded or proven incorrect
- Initiatives that are no longer being pursued

## What Does NOT Go Here

- Work that is paused but may resume — keep in its original location
- Reference material that is still valid — keep in the relevant knowledge folder
- Historical records needed for Decision Log context — keep in place
