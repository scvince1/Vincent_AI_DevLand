# Nutrition for Hypertrophy + Climbing Recomp (91kg/173cm)

## Daily Targets (Recomp, Not Bulk)
- **Calories:** ~2,400-2,600 kcal/day (maintenance or slight deficit -100 to -200)
- **Protein:** 180-200g/day (1.8-2.2 g/kg; spread across 4 meals of ~40-50g)
- **Fat:** 70-85g/day (0.8-0.9 g/kg; supports hormones and joint health)
- **Carbs:** 250-300g/day (fill remainder; fuel climbing and compounds)

Key insight: at current training age and body fat level, recomp works. No caloric surplus needed for muscle gain — need adequate protein + progressive overload.

## Day-Type Nutrition Cycling
| | Climbing Day | Strength Day | Rest Day |
|---|---|---|---|
| Calories | ~2,500 (maintenance) | ~2,600 (maintenance +100) | ~2,300 (maintenance -200) |
| Carbs | Higher (300g+) | Moderate-high (270g) | Lower (200g) |
| Protein | 180g | 200g | 180g |
| Fat | Lower (~65g) | Moderate (~75g) | Higher (~85g) |
| Timing | Light meal 2-3hr pre-climb; fast carbs intra if >90min | Standard pre/post workout | No special timing |

Pre-climb: small, carb-focused (rice, banana), not high-fat. Climbing performance suffers from feeling heavy/bloated.

## Meal Prep Tracking Priorities
**Must track:** protein (weigh raw), total calories (rough but honest)
**Track if easy:** carbs and fats (helps with day-cycling)
**Don't bother:** daily micronutrients. Eat vegetables, take multivitamin, supplement creatine 5g/day and vitamin D.

## Takeout Estimation Heuristics (Chinese/Asian Food)
- Rice: 1 takeout container ≈ 300g cooked ≈ 350 kcal, 6g protein
- Stir-fried meat dishes: assume 30% is oil/sauce, typical plate = 400-600 kcal
- Deep fried: multiply estimate by 1.5× calories
- Sauced dishes (mapo tofu, kung pao): +150-200 kcal for oil
- Soup noodles: broth-based ≈ 500 kcal; oily (lanzhou, dan dan) ≈ 700-900 kcal
- **General rule: estimate then add 20%** (Asian restaurant cooking uses more oil than home)
- **Protein hack:** order plain steamed/grilled protein side to guarantee hitting 180g
