# 88_Learned
## Vincent's Learned Knowledge Base

External knowledge Vincent has read, studied, and internalized. Not his original ideas, but the raw material his thinking draws on.

### Structure
- One .md file per concept, framework, or factual finding
- Files are small, modular, and self-contained
- Each file has frontmatter: source, date learned, related projects/articles

### What goes here
- Concepts and frameworks from other people's articles, books, papers
- Empirical data and statistics with sources (e.g. McKinsey surveys)
- Historical case studies and examples
- Notes from things Vincent has read or studied
- External material that arrived via Dump and was processed

### What does NOT go here
- Vincent's own original ideas (those live in articles, observations, or intellectual themes)
- People information (87_People)
- System configuration (85_System, 86_AI_Systems)

### Frontmatter template
```yaml
---
concept: [name of concept or finding]
author: [original author(s)]
source_title: [book, article, or report title]
source_date: [original publication year]
logged: [date Vincent learned or recorded this]
tags: [fine-grained topic tags for retrieval]
related: [path/to/article-or-project.md]
---
```
