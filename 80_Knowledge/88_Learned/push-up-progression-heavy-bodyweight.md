# Push-Up Progression at 91kg with TFCC

## Effective Load
- Standard push-up at 91kg ≈ 58-63kg on hands (64-69% BW)
- Kneeling push-up ≈ 45-49kg (49-54% BW) — equivalent to ~50kg bench press

## TFCC Protection: CRITICAL
- Standard flat-hand push-up = ~70° wrist dorsiflexion → directly loads TFCC
- **Solution: Dumbbell push-ups (12lb DBs on floor)** = neutral wrist position
- Alternatives: knuckle push-ups on mat, push-up handles/parallettes
- CONSTRAINT: "Push-ups default to neutral wrist (dumbbells/handles), flat-hand prohibited"

## Push-ups Alone = Sufficient for Pec Hypertrophy
- Kotarsky et al. (2018, JSCR): Push-up group +18.3% pec thickness vs bench press +19.4% (equivalent)
- Weekly volume needed: 12-18 working sets chest, 3x/week frequency

## Progression Path (RIR 2-3, no failure required)
1. Phase 1: 4-5 sets × 8 standard (kneeling to fill if needed)
2. Add 1 set every 1-2 weeks until 5×10 clean
3. Phase 2: Elevate feet, or add weight
4. Phase 3: Archer push-ups, decline variations

## Sandbag Floor Press as Supplement
- 45kg load, neutral grip, but limited ROM (upper arms touch floor)
- Combine with push-ups: push-ups for ROM/stretch stimulus + floor press for load variety
