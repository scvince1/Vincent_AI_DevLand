# Pistol Squat Progression + Functional Carrying Strength

## Pistol Squat Prerequisites (at 91kg)
- **Ankle dorsiflexion:** Knee-to-wall test, 4-5" from wall (40-45°). If failing, pistol bottom position is mechanically impossible without heel wedge
- **Hip flexion:** 120+ degrees passive ROM + active hip flexor strength to hold non-working leg extended
- **Single-leg strength:** 5 clean single-leg box squats (knee-height) per side
- **Balance:** 30-second single-leg stand with eyes closed

## The 91kg Challenge
- Pistol squat = ~90kg single-leg squat through extreme ROM
- Joint stress on knee (meniscus compression in deep flexion) is the real limiter at heavier bodyweight
- Old knee injuries increase risk
- **Skater squat** may be safer long-term alternative (rear knee toward floor instead of full pistol depth)

## Progression Path (8-16 weeks)
1. Weeks 1-4: Ankle mobility daily + Bulgarian split squats 3×8 + single-leg box squats to knee height
2. Weeks 5-8: Lower box progressively + TRX/band-assisted pistols + heel-elevated pistol negatives (3-5s eccentric)
3. Weeks 9-12: Counterbalanced pistols (hold 5-10kg plate for balance) + reduce assistance
4. Weeks 13-16: Full unassisted pistol squats, low volume (2-3 reps/set)

## Hamstring Pain from Deadlifts
- 10 sets × 10-12 reps = 100-120 total reps is extreme deadlift volume
- Multi-day pain is almost certainly excessive volume DOMS (if bilateral, diffuse, peaks 24-72hr)
- **Fix: cut to 4-5 working sets.** Soreness will likely resolve
- If sharp, unilateral, or persists >5 days → assess for proximal hamstring tendinopathy

## Functional Carrying Benchmarks (Dan John standards)
| Lift | Target | Current | Gap |
|------|--------|---------|-----|
| Deadlift 1RM | ~135kg (1.5× BW) | ~85-90kg est. | Significant |
| Farmer carry | 45kg/hand × 100m | Unknown | Build up |
| Front-loaded carry | 60kg × 50m | Unknown | Direct goal |

## Key Exercises for Carrying Strength
- **Sandbag carries** (bear hug, shoulder, zercher) — most transferable for carrying a person
- **Farmer walks** — 2-3×/week, build from 30kg/hand×40m to 45kg/hand×100m
- **Zercher squats** — barbell in elbow crooks, mimics holding a person, 40-60kg × 3×6-8
- **Romanian deadlifts** — lighter, controlled hip extension power
- **Loaded step-ups and walking lunges**
- **Turkish get-ups** — floor-to-standing under load

## Programming Shift Needed
- Current 10×10-12 is high volume / moderate intensity — wrong for functional strength
- Shift to: heavier loads (3-6 reps), fewer sets (3-5), dedicated carry work 2×/week as finishers
