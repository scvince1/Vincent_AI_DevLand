# Mixed-Modality Training Load: Foster Method

## The Formula
Session Load (AU) = Session RPE (Borg CR10, 0-10) × Duration (minutes)
Collect sRPE 15-30 minutes post-session.

## Validated for Non-Traditional Modalities
- Haddad et al. (2020, Frontiers in Physiology, PMID: 32903483): sRPE superior to TRIMP for functional fitness training
- Validated for HIFT formats structurally similar to Animal Flow + clubbell + boxing combos
- Mingels et al. (2023, BMC Sports Science): criterion-related validity confirmed across diverse modalities

## Weekly Load Management
- Weekly Load = sum of all session AUs (unified pool, don't separate modalities)
- Training Monotony = weekly mean AU / SD of daily AU → keep <2.0
- Training Strain = weekly load × monotony
- Varied modalities naturally reduce monotony (advantage of multi-modal training)

## Practical Application
| Session Type | Example sRPE | Duration | AU |
|---|---|---|---|
| Heavy strength | 8 | 45 min | 360 |
| Animal Flow + clubbell combo | 7 | 40 min | 280 |
| Climbing session | 7 | 90 min | 630 |
| Boxing follow-along | 6 | 30 min | 180 |
| Rest day | 0 | 0 | 0 |
