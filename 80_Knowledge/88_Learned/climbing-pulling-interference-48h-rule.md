# Climbing-Pulling Interference: The 48-Hour Rule

## Verdict: Reasonable extrapolation, NOT directly validated
No study directly tests climbing + pull-day sequencing at any interval.

## Best Available Evidence
- Heid et al. (2024, Frontiers in Sports and Active Living): Elite bouldering competitors. Grip strength recovered by 24h. Subjective readiness not restored until 60h. But this was competition intensity.
- Hernández-Belmonte et al. (2023, Physical Therapy in Sport): Advanced climbers recovered FASTER than intermediates.
- Saeterbakken et al. (2024, Sports Medicine Open): Recommends 2x/week resistance training for climbers but provides no sequencing protocols.
