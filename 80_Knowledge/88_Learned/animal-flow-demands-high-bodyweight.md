# Animal Flow at High Bodyweight: Genuine Training Stimulus

## EMG Data (2024 Physical Therapy in Sport, PMID: 39593581)
- Medial deltoid: 66.58% MVIC (vs push-up 45.63%)
- Latissimus dorsi: 62.81% MVIC (vs push-up 29.17%)
- Biceps brachii: 47.72% MVIC (vs push-up 23.31%)
- Biceps femoris: 41.89% MVIC (vs squat 17.79%)

## Metabolic Demand
- Mean 5.4 METs, peak 7.0 METs (Buxton 2022, Frontiers)
- At 91kg: ~8.0-8.5 kcal/min absolute EE (vs ~6.7 in study subjects at ~70-75kg)
- HR reaches 153 bpm in peak flow segments
- ~60% of class time classified as vigorous intensity

## Classification: NOT Recovery/Mobility
Correct classification: Stability + Strength-Endurance + Metabolic Conditioning
- Each quadrupedal position places 50-65kg through the upper kinetic chain at 91kg
- Heavy individuals who cannot flow smoothly → higher anaerobic contribution
- Treat as upper body resistance session for recovery planning

## Recovery Implication
- 24-48h before next upper-body-intensive session
- Combined with clubbell/boxing = high-load conditioning day, 48h+ recovery
