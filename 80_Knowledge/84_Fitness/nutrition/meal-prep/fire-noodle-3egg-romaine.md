## Agent Notes

- **Protein: 37g** -- above 30g floor but below 50g target. Adding a 4th egg would bring it to ~43g.
- **Sodium: 1,871mg (full sauce)** -- this single meal is 81% of the 2,300mg UL. With half sauce, drops to ~1,300mg (57% UL).
- **Cholesterol: 563mg** -- high from 3 eggs. USDA removed dietary cholesterol limit in 2015-2020 DGA, but still noteworthy.
- **Romaine provides excellent potassium** (865mg) partially offsetting the Na bomb from the noodles.
- **Na:K ratio: 1.42 (full sauce) / 0.98 (half sauce)** -- half sauce version achieves near-balanced Na:K.
