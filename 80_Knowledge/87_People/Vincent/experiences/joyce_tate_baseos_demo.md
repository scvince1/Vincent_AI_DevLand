## Key Insights Extracted
- Human sloppiness (audience's and Vincent's own) is the first design constraint for any AI-facing product. "The Alignment Tax" article draws directly from this moment.
- You can ship a beautiful system and still lose the room if you don't prep the floor beneath the floor — the shared vocabulary and mental model required to even evaluate what's on screen.
- Teaching (Tate) is a stress test for understanding. If you can hand someone else the blueprint and watch them build, you know the design actually factored correctly.
